# Project Rules

## Project
Crucial Recycling website

## Core goal
Build a clean, mobile-first, conversion-focused local service website for a rubbish removal and house clearance business in the West Midlands.

## Business positioning
- Licensed
- Fast
- Eco-friendly
- Cheaper than a skip

## Primary conversions
1. WhatsApp enquiry
2. Phone call
3. Quote request with photo upload

## Phase 1 only
- Home
- Services
- House Clearance
- Bulky Waste & Furniture Collection
- Garden, Shed & Garage Clearance
- Commercial Waste & Cardboard Collection
- How It Works
- Areas Served
- About
- Reviews
- FAQ
- Get a Quote
- Legal pages

## Hard rules
- Use Next.js App Router
- Use TypeScript
- Use Tailwind CSS
- Keep components reusable and simple
- No backend unless explicitly requested
- No CMS yet
- No booking system yet
- No dummy feature bloat
- No fake business claims
- No invented testimonials
- No fake pricing
- No overdesigned startup visuals
- Keep copy lean and replaceable
- Prioritize mobile CTA visibility
- Prioritize local SEO structure
- Prioritize trust markers early on page

## Design direction
- Clean
- Sturdy
- Trustworthy
- Local-service confidence
- Deep green, charcoal, off-white
- Strong sans-serif headings
- High-contrast CTAs

## Important UX requirements
- Sticky mobile CTA
- Click-to-call
- WhatsApp CTA
- Quote CTA on major pages
- Trust strip high on page
- Clear domestic/commercial separation where relevant

## Homepage requirements
- Hero
- Trust strip
- Core service cards
- Why choose us
- How it works
- Areas served
- Reviews
- FAQ
- Final CTA band

## Content rules
- Do not invent business facts
- Do not invent service areas beyond confirmed West Midlands direction
- Do not invent review counts or ratings
- Use placeholders where real business data is missing
- Structure for SEO and conversion first
